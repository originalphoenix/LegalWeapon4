What is LEGALEASE?

only with understanding can we have agreement.

How many times a day do you click that little "I Agree" checkbox? And how many times do you actually read what you're agreeing to? We're willing to bet that the only peeople that actually read EULAs (end-user license agreements), Terms and Conditions, and Privacy Policies are the poor lawyers whose jobs it is to write the things.

Ain't nobody got time for that - we've got better things to do on the Internet. Cat videos, for one.

Unfortunately, what you don't know does hurt you. Who owns those red-cup photos you posted to your Instagram account last night? Are you allowed to use Pandora as background ambience at your mom-and-pop cafe? Did you see something about "nuclear weapons" in that iTunes agreement, or were you just skimming too fast? (you weren't.)

LEGALEASE is here to help you blast through the legal speak, and output a clean, simple document that even a baby could understand (if babies could read). Simply copy/paste your document into the window, and LEGALEASE will do the rest.
